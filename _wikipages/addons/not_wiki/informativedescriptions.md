      BetterHealthUI adds a full overhaul of the health UI: clearer at a glance, configurable, less clutter, more useful during combat and treatment.

      #### Load Order:
      - This mod does **not** have to be loaded above Neurotrauma itself.
      <br><br>

      #### Compatibility:
      - Has support for: Base Neurotrauma, NT: Cybernetics, NT: Cybernetics Enhanced, NT: Cybernetics Improved.
      - Can sometimes crash in the Submarine Editor if too many mods are loaded.
      <br><br>
