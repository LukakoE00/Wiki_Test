      An addon to Neurotrauma that overhauls the infection system; adding several new items, infections and drugs to deal with them.

      #### Load Order:
      - This mod does **not** have to be loaded above Neurotrauma itself.
      <br><br>

      #### Compatibility:
      - If NT: Surgery Plus (or an equivalent mod) is loaded, NT: Infections **must** be loaded below it.
      <br><br>
