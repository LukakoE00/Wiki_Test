    header: "Caused By:"
    order: 2
    text: |
     - {{HYPERVENTILATION}}
     - {{VOMITING}}
     - {{BLOOD_PACKS}} who's donor had alkalosis (alkalosis gained will be the donor's alkalosis level at time of extraction divided by 5)
  
  - type: effects
    header: "Effects:"
    order: 3
    text: |
     - {{SEIZURE}} (at 60% or more)
     - {{PALPITATIONS}} (at 20% or more)
  
  - type: treatments
    header: "Treatments:"
    order: 4
    text: |
     - Treating the causes
     - Inducing {{ACIDOSIS}} (preferably with {{SALINE}})
     - Transfusing neutral {{BLOOD_PACKS}} (unless the patient has 0% blood loss)
     - {{DIALYSIS_FILTER}}
