     Hypoxemia is the absence of adequate oxygen in the bloodstream. Hypoxemia can quickly build up and cause the patient to spiral out of control. A patient suffering from this needs immediate medical care. {{PNEUMOTHORAX}} will prevent Hypoxemia from going down, proportional to its strength.
