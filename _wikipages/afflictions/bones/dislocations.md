    text: |
     Dislocations are common injuries, which most crewmembers will get often. Dislocations will not kill, and should be treated last in an emergency. A limb can be both dislocated and fractured. Cyberlimbs cannot be dislocated. The limb locking and character slowdown can be prevented with {{ADRENALINE}}.
  
  - type: caused_by
    header: "Caused By:"
    order: 2
    text: |
     - Trauma (explosions, blunt force, internal damage)
  
  - type: effects
    header: "Effects:"
    order: 3
    text: |
     ### In leg dislocations:

     - 1 leg dislocation will halve speed
     - 2 leg dislocations will make you crawl.
     - {{INTENSE_PAIN}}
     - {{INTERNAL_WOUNDS}}, if not {{BANDAGED}} or {{ANALGESIA}}

     ### In arm dislocations:

     - 1 arm dislocation will prevent you from using that arm (you cant hold 2-handed items anymore)
     - 2 arm dislocations will prevent you from interacting with anything, such as doors, ladders, buttons, etc.
     - {{INTENSE_PAIN}}
     - {{INTERNAL_WOUNDS}}, if not {{BANDAGED}} or {{ANALGESIA}}
  
  - type: treatments
    header: "Treatments:"
    order: 4
    text: |
     - {{WRENCH}} (use it in the health interface, like you would a bandage)
       The skill check is 60 medical without and 30 medical with {{ANALGESIA}}
  
  - type: first_aid
    header: "First Aid:"
    order: 5
    text: |
     - {{BANDAGES}} or {{PLASTISEAL}}
