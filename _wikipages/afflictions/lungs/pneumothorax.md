    text: |
     Pneumothorax will prevent {{HYPOXEMIA}} from decreasing in strength. While not lethal on its own, Pneumothorax can easily kill if paired with afflictions that cause Hypoxemia. Pneumothorax's negative effects can be temporarily negated by using a {{NEEDLE}}.
  
  - type: caused_by
    header: "Caused By:"
    order: 2
    text: |
     - {{FRACTURES}} (rib fractures, if they arent bandaged)
     - Trauma (gunshots, explosions, lacerations, bite wounds, blunt force, {{INTERNAL_DAMAGE}})
     - {{NEEDLE}}
  
  - type: effects
    header: "Effects:"
    order: 3
    text: |
     - {{SHORTNESS_OF_BREATH}} (at 40% or more)
     - {{HYPERVENTILATION}} (at 15% or more)
     - {{INCREASED_HEARTRATE}} (at 30% or more)
     - Prevents {{HYPOXEMIA}} from decreasing in severity, proportional to the strength of the affliction
  
  - type: treatments
    header: "Treatments:"
    order: 4
    text: |
     - Using a {{DRAINAGE}} during surgery (After {{SKIN_RETRACTORS}})
  
  - type: first_aid
    header: "First Aid:"
    order: 5
    text: |
     - {{NEEDLE}}
