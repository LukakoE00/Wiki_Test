    text: |
     The first step in **all** surgical procedures. Analgesia is an affliction that prevents traumatic shock during surgery and relieves pain. Traumatic shock will still occur if surgery is performed without a table. If strong enough, analgesia will also provide damage resistance and stun resistance.
  
  - type: caused_by
    header: "Caused By:"
    order: 2
    text: |
     - {{OPIOIDS}}
     - {{ADRENALINE}} (doesn't give more than 4.5% analgesia)
     - {{SPINAL_CORD_INJURY}}
     - {{DRUNK}} (20% or more, doesn't give more than 5% analgesia)
     - {{ANESTHESIA}} (doesn't give more than 5% analgesia)
  
  - type: effects
    header: "Effects:"
    order: 3
    text: |
     - {{HYPOVENTILATION}} (at 20% or more)
     - 25% damage resistance (at 20% or more)
     - 25% {{STUN}} resistance (at 20% or more)
     - Removes wobbly aim when arms are damaged (at 20% or more)
     - Prevents extremity {{FRACTURES}} and {{DISLOCATIONS}} from causing {{INTERNAL_WOUNDS}}
     - Reduces the {{WRENCH}} skillcheck from 60 to 30 medical
     - Allows for surgery tools to be used
  
  - type: treatments
    header: "Treatments:"
    order: 4
    text: |
     - {{NALOXONE}}
