    text: |
     Burns can be subdivided into three categories; first, second and third degree - with severe cases having different treatment methods respectively. First degree burns are generally minor and can (usually) be ignored though second and third degrees require direct medical intervention.

     Burns between 1% to 20% strength are first degree, between 21% to 50% they are second degree and above 51% they are third degree. Both acid burns and normal burns are counted together when determining the actual degree.

     It's important to note that - unlike other traumatic afflictions that directly affect vitality - burns will never appear on the {{HEALTH_SCANNER}}.

     - May cause {{INFECTED_WOUNDS}}, depending on burn severity.
