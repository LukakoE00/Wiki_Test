     \-8% {{LIVER_DAMAGE}}
     \-8% {{VANILLA_ORGAN_DAMAGE}}

     Grants +50% Thiamine, which treats the above organ damage over 200 seconds.

     ### Application failure:

     \-4.8% {{LUNG_DAMAGE}}
     \-4.8% {{HEART_DAMAGE}}
     \-4.8% {{KIDNEY_DAMAGE}}
     \-4.8% {{LIVER_DAMAGE}}

     Grants +30% Thiamine, which treats the above organ damage over 120 seconds.
