     A medic's best friend. To use the autopulse, simply wear it in your diving suit slot. While worn and equipped with batteries, the autopulse will cure {{CARDIAC_ARREST}} and slightly treat {{OXYGEN_LOW}} and {{FIBRILLATION}}, as well as increasing your blood pressure. Be wary to not overuse it, however, as it will increase your blood pressure into {{HYPERTENSION}} . It can be forcibly removed by using the {{WRENCH}} as a treatment on the torso of a person wearing it, as long as the person is not {{UNCONSCIOUSNESS}}. Doing so will place it in the inventory of the wrench user.
