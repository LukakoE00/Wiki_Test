     The Body Bag is an item used to transport bodies much more quickly than simply dragging them. It is placed into the Diving Suit slot and will suffocate a living human if they are placed into it. It can be forcibly removed by using the {{WRENCH}} as a treatment on the torso of a person wearing it, as long as the person is not {{UNCONSCIOUSNESS}}. Doing so will place it in the inventory of the wrench user.
