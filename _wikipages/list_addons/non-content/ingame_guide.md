      The In-Game Guide adds a browsable guide akin to the Wiki / Trello containing information, as well as integrating into the Health Scanner / Hematology Analyzer and the Health Menu.

      <br>
      #### Load Order:
      - This mod does **not** have to be loaded above Neurotrauma itself.
      
      <br>
      #### Compatibility:
      - Has support for: 
        - Base Neurotrauma
        - NT: Cybernetics Enhanced
        - NT: Surgery Plus
        - NT: Eyes
        - NT: Infections
        - NT: Grafting
        - NT: Lobotomy
        - NT: Thermal
        - NT: Airways
        - NT: Pharmacy (Calculator only)
