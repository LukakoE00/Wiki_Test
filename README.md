### A work-in-progress GitHub pages website that can hopefully replace the Neurotrauma Trello in terms of primary information source

Now you might ask yourself: why would you do this? Simple! Trello is unwieldy, hard to navigate for new people and don't even begin to attempt to open it on your phone. It was selected years ago as the go-to place for the Wiki and now we're stuck in the sunk-cost fallacy phase of it all. 

No longer!
Jekyll and Liquid make this relatively 'easy' (though this is still a mess don't get me wrong) to expand and maintain.

If you wish to add content, all you need to do is update or make a new .md (markdown) file, look at the other files for usable elements and fill them in! The rest of the work is done automatically.

#### Adding non-page content
If you want to add images or new templates, just shove them into the folders that already contain those elements; it's quite easy.

#### Featuring
- A search function, powered by Lunr.
- Indexed pages for browsing (and a home page!)
- Mobile device support
- Easy expandability via PRs
- My sanity
